Word Inventory

The Word Inventory analysis calculates a word inventory and token summary for each selected participant.

Parameters

By default, the word inventory analysis will create an inventory of words in the Orthogrpahy tier. You may change this using the form shown above. To add additional tiers, use the Add Aligned Words field under Word Options.

Other Parameters

  • Ignore diacritics - ignore diacritics when comparing phones (default: no)
